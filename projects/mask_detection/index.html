Mask detection with ml5.js

Step 1: Give permission to your browser to access your camera and click on the canvas. Then, create the dataset by moving your face in different places and angles through the canvas:

  • Wear your mask and press 'm' to add examples with mask.
  • Remove your mask and press 'n' to add examples without mask.
  • Note: Try to add as many examples as you can so the neural network has enough data for training.
  • IMPORTANT: there must be similar numbers of examples for each class, else it will create an unbalanced dataset (normally about 100 for each class is enough, although it is not a fixed number since it depends on the quality of the camera, the brightness of the room ...).

Step 2: Press 't' to train your neural network. The neural network will start training and you can visualize the loss (which must be decreasing).

  • Note: Depending on the browser, when starting the training, the browser may hang. Please wait patiently. Training may take a while.

Step 3: Once it is trained (50 epochs), click the 'Hide' button.

Step 4: Test your neural network!

Note: If it doesn't classify correctly, probabily it needed more examples or the examples taken are not varied enough. You can restart the sketch to create a new neural network.
